The Indestructible Mind

About Powers

Superhero Name: The Mind
Superhero Status: Category B
Superhero Power: Psychokinesis
Backstory: The Mind was a mere 16-year old girl when she was abducted by aliens, of whom performed experiments on her unconscious human mind. When she awoke, she used her unlocked powers to break out of the ship, killing all on board. Now since found in a forest, she intends to wipe out all evil and find her true purpose.
